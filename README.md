## 7. 3-Minute Pitch

### 7.1 Problem

Many freelancers, small business owners, and early-stage entrepreneurs struggle to establish a professional online presence. They either lack the technical skills to build modern websites, can't afford expensive agencies, or end up with generic templates that don't reflect their unique brand. This digital gap limits their ability to attract clients, grow revenue, and compete in an increasingly online-first economy.

### 7.2 Customer Segments

- **Freelancers & independent professionals** (developers, designers, consultants, artists)
- **Local small businesses** lacking web presence (restaurants, salons, repair shops)
- **Nonprofits and early-stage entrepreneurs** with limited budgets
- **Bootcamp graduates** needing portfolios to land their first jobs

### 7.3 Unique Value Proposition

DevArts combines **full-stack web development expertise** with **AI-powered content tools** to deliver high-quality, personalized digital solutions at accessible price points. Unlike DIY builders, we offer custom design and real developer support. Unlike agencies, we're affordable and fast. We bridge the gap â€” professional results without the enterprise cost.

### 7.4 Key Partners

- **Local business associations & chambers of commerce** (referral pipelines)
- **Coding bootcamps & workforce development programs** (graduate portfolio services)
- **AI/SaaS tool providers** (OpenAI, Vercel, Stripe for integrated solutions)
- **Freelance networks & creative communities** (co-marketing, subcontracting)
