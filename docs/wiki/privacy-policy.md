# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** February 1, 2026

DevArts Lab ("we," "our," or "us") operates digital services, including devartslab.com and related properties (the "Services"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe.

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Contact information:** name, email address, phone number, company, job title.
- **Payment information:** billing address and transaction details processed via third-party processors (e.g., Stripe). We do not store full payment card numbers.
- **Usage data:** log data, device/browser information, IP address, pages viewed, and interactions.
- **Support communications:** messages and attachments you send to support.

## 2. How We Use Information
- Provide, maintain, and improve the Services.
- Process transactions and send invoices/receipts.
- Respond to support requests and communicate service updates.
- Personalize content and measure performance.
- Detect, prevent, and address fraud or abuse.
- Comply with legal obligations.

## 3. Sharing and Disclosure
- **Service providers:** infrastructure, analytics, payment processing, email delivery, and customer support tools under confidentiality obligations.
- **Legal and safety:** when required by law or to protect rights, property, and safety.
- **Business transfers:** as part of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, with notice as required by law.

## 4. Cookies and Tracking
We use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences, maintain sessions, and understand usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling cookies may limit functionality.

## 5. Data Retention
We keep information as long as necessary to provide the Services, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes. When no longer needed, we delete or anonymize data.

## 6. Security
We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards (including encryption in transit, access controls, and monitoring) to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; please use strong passwords and protect your account.

## 7. Your Choices
- Opt out of marketing emails via unsubscribe links (transactional emails will continue).
- Update or request deletion of your information by contacting us at the email below.

## 8. International Transfers
Information may be processed in countries different from yours. We take steps to ensure appropriate protections consistent with applicable law.

## 9. Children
Our Services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child provided information, contact us to remove it.

## 10. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy periodically. Changes take effect when posted to this page. We will notify you of significant changes as required by law.
